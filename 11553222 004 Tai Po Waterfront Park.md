Tai Po Waterfront Park is located on Tai Fat Street, Tai Po, Tai Po District, New Territories, Hong Kong. It is adjacent to Tai Po Innovation Park, Fu Shin Village and Kwong Fuk Village. It faces Yuen Chau Tsai Park in the south and is separated by the estuary of Lam Tsuen River.  Tai Po Waterfront Park was built on approximately 22 hectares of land reclaimed from land.
